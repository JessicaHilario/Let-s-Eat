Lets Eat! User Manual

What is this:

Let’s eat is an app that allows you to randomly generate restaurants based your preferences From system vision: The Let’s Eat! application purpose is to help people choose what restaurant they should go to. It will create a list of restaurants based on the filters the user has put in. Our application’s features will reduce the frustration of finding somewhere to eat, encourages users to experience somewhere new, and requires less memory space since the directions will pop up on the application as well. The main elements are the end users and the system because it is the basis of what our application is trying to accomplish.


Getting Started

Making an Account:

1. From the Home Page, select the Sign In/Sign Up option, and select Create an Account
2. Provide your email address, create a password for your account, and provide any other requested information
3. An email will be sent to you to confirm your new account


Finding A Restaurant

1. Select generate restaurant (or whatever the name of this will be)
2. Enter your preference filters
3. Select Find a Restaurant
4. The resulting restaurant will be presented to you with its basic information
5. You may choose to view the restaurant’s Yelp page from within the app by selecting the View Yelp Page button
6. You may also get directions via Google Maps from within the app by selecting the Get Directions button


Group Voting

1. If you wish to start a group vote with friends, select the Group Vote option from the Home Page
2. To add people to the group vote, a group invite code will be generated which you can share with the people you wish to
include in the vote. If you are friends with someone you wish to add to the vote, you can select them from the Friends List on the side, and they will be invited to to group
3. If you receive a group invite code, you can join the group by selecting the Join Group option from the Group Vote page
4. Once all the group members have joined, select the Start Group Vote option
5. Each member will be prompted to enter their preferred restaurant filters
6. There will be a countdown timer on the vote page, which indicates the time left to apply filters
7. Once the Find a Restaurant button is selected, or the time expires, all of the current filters will be taken, and a matching restaurant will be selected
8. The chosen restaurant will be presented to all group members, each of which can either choose to View the Yelp Page or Get Directions


Adding Friends

1. You may add friends within the app by selecting the Add Friends option from the Home Page
2. You can look up friends by using the email they used to sign up for their account
3. If the user you search for is found, you can send the a friend request, which they will be notified of


Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is this a free service
A: There is no charge to use this system. You will not be asked to provide any payment information

Q: Do you share our data with any businesses?
A: Privacy is important to us; all of your data is encrypted and stored securely in our system. We do not send, share, or sell any of your information with any restaurants or other businesses or organizations.

Q: Can businesses advertise on the app?
A: Businesses can choose to advertise their businesses on the app, however we will not share any of your information with them
